How evolved is your organisation when it comes to Social Value?

1. To what extent are your mission, vision and values aligned to supporting people, place and planet?

2. How involved with communities and causes is your business / your staff?

3. How clearly do you quantify the benefit of your policies and actions towards people and planet?

4. To what extent do you take decisions on which suppliers to engage, based on their attitudes and delivery to the greater good of the community and the planet?

5. How well do you understand which actions are most likely to have the greatest positive impact on people and planet?
